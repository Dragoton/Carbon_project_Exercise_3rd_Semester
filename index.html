About the project

The Green Click Movement is a non-profit project aimed at spreading global awareness of reducing carbon emissions by optimizing websites and applications.

1.Test your website

By testing, you get information on how much electricity your site consumes and how much of that energy is from renewable sources, and its responsibility for CO2 emissions.

2.Improve your page

After testing your developer will receive all the information on how to improve and optimize the website, as well as advisory assistance in solving optimization processes.

3. Earn a "green badge"

The green results of your site will be awarded a green badge by which your companie / organization can prove Environmental Awareness of and on your website.
